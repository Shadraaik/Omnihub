Teleter
Teleter is a LIVE video chatting platform that enables direct, real-time paid interaction between users and celebrities, influencers, and notable figures, bridging the gap between fans and the elites through secure and interactive video sessions.

It combines “Tele” from Television and “ter” from Letter to create a unique way to send real-time messages. Whether you want information, advice, or just to say hello, Teleter makes it easy to have personal conversations with your favorite stars.

Teleter Terminology

Telequest – A request sent to someone on Teleter to have a live video chat. It’s how you ask to connect with a celeb, influencer, or expert in real time.

Telete(s) – The digital coins or credits used on the Teleter platform. Teletes power your conversations and are how you unlock time with the people you admire.

Founded on the 06th, July 2025 by Shadrack Yarah I, who serves as the CEO of Teleter.
